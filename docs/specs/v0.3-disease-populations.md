# Harmonia — design spec extension v0.3: disease & genetic population backgrounds

**Status:** **implemented in v0.3.0** (2026-06-09) · extends [`spec.md`](../../spec.md) (v0.1)
· completes the **disease & genetic backgrounds** half of the `populations` subsystem
(spec.md §3) · **ships hypothesis-tier, NOT FOR PREDICTION, and relaxes no §10 guardrail.**

> *v0.1's `populations` subsystem propagated inter-individual **variability** (a cloud of
> conductance CVs around the healthy mean). It never represented the other half the spec
> §3 names: a **disease or genetic background** — a systematic *shift* of a current, not a
> spread. v0.3 adds that shift as a first-class, optional field, and ships the three
> canonical congenital long-QT channelopathies (LQT1/2/3) as population records, so a
> drug's risk distribution can be re-evaluated against a reduced-repolarization-reserve
> background. It remains exactly as non-predictive as the v0.1 populations layer.*

---

## 0. Why this, and why now

The load-bearing clinical fact behind drug-induced torsade is **repolarization reserve**
([Moss & Kass 2005](../../dataset/citations/moss-2005.json)): the healthy ventricle
repolarizes through several redundant currents, so blocking one (e.g. IKr) is usually
tolerated. A patient with a *latent channelopathy* — a congenital loss of IKs (LQT1),
IKr (LQT2), or a gain of late sodium current (LQT3) — has already spent that reserve, and
the *same* drug block that is benign in a healthy myocyte can be torsadogenic against the
disease background. This is precisely the gene–drug interaction that makes some drugs
dangerous only in susceptible subpopulations.

v0.1 shipped the *machinery* for population spread but only one **illustrative
variability** population (`illustrative_v0`: conductance CVs, no mean shift). The disease
half — the spec.md §3 promise of "disease & genetic backgrounds" — was unimplemented. v0.3
closes it with the minimal, honest addition: a per-current **mean multiplier**.

---

## 1. Scope

**In scope**

- A backward-compatible **`conductance_scale`** field on a `population` record: a per-current
  *mean* multiplier (e.g. `{"IKr": 0.5}` halves the IKr conductance for every virtual myocyte
  in the population) applied *underneath* the existing `conductance_cv` inter-individual spread.
- Three congenital long-QT **disease population records** — LQT1 (IKs↓), LQT2 (IKr↓),
  LQT3 (INaL↑) — each citing the channelopathy mechanism and carrying the project's
  non-predictive labelling.
- The `assess_population` drop-in: the disease mean shift composes with the CV spread and
  the drug block, so a drug's classification distribution is re-evaluated against the
  reduced-reserve background. No new API surface — `harmonia population <drug> --population lqt2`.

**Out of scope (unchanged from v0.1 §2)**

- Any **predictive** claim. The disease magnitudes are *illustrative heterozygous-scale*
  reductions chosen to demonstrate the mechanism, **not** patient- or genotype-calibrated
  parameters. Every disease-population assessment is **Tier D** and stamped NOT FOR PREDICTION,
  exactly like `illustrative_v0`.
- De novo channelopathy biophysics. We scale the published conductances; we do not re-derive
  mutant channel kinetics (a mutation-specific Markov model is a separate, later thread).
- Acquired/drug-induced reserve loss beyond what the existing drug-block layer already models.

---

## 2. The model — a mean shift under the existing spread

The v0.1 population draws, per virtual myocyte *m* and current *c*, a lognormal multiplier
`λ_{m,c} ~ LogNormal(0, σ_c)` with `σ_c` from `conductance_cv` (mean ≈ 1, i.e. centered on the
healthy conductance). v0.3 multiplies that draw by the **disease mean shift** `s_c` from
`conductance_scale` (default 1):

```
g_{m,c} = s_c · λ_{m,c} · g_c^{healthy}
```

So a disease population is "the healthy variability cloud, recentered on the diseased mean."
LQT2 (`s_IKr = 0.5`) is a population whose every myocyte has ~half the IKr it otherwise would,
still with the inter-individual spread around that reduced level. The drug block multiplies in
on top, unchanged. The qNet / ΔAPD90 classifier thresholds are the **healthy** reference (they
were calibrated on the healthy baseline), which is exactly the point: a disease background that
pushes qNet across the healthy threshold is the quantitative statement "this background has
spent its reserve."

The illustrative magnitudes (v0.3):

| Population | Gene (mechanism) | Current shift | `conductance_scale` |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `lqt1` | *KCNQ1* loss of function | IKs ↓ ~50% | `{"IKs": 0.5}` |
| `lqt2` | *KCNH2* / hERG loss of function | IKr ↓ ~50% | `{"IKr": 0.5}` |
| `lqt3` | *SCN5A* gain of function | late INa (INaL) ↑ ~100% | `{"INaL": 2.0}` |

These are heterozygous-scale, order-of-magnitude-honest illustrations of the dominant defect,
not genotype-specific values. They reproduce the textbook ordering in the reduced kernel —
LQT2 prolongs the AP most (direct IKr loss), LQT3 lowers qNet most (the inward-current shift) —
which is the validation that the *mechanism*, not a tuned number, is doing the work.

## 3. Schema delta (backward-compatible)

One new optional property on the `population` object; every existing population record
validates unchanged.

```jsonc
"population": {
  "name": "Congenital LQT2 (KCNH2/hERG loss of function) — illustrative",
  "n_default": 100,
  "conductance_cv":    { "IKr": 0.22, "IKs": 0.28, "ICaL": 0.18, "INaL": 0.25, ... },
  "conductance_scale": { "IKr": 0.5 },     // NEW (optional) — disease MEAN multiplier, default 1
  "predictive": false                       // still const false — never predictive
}
```

`conductance_scale` is validated as `channel → positive number`. A population with no
`conductance_scale` (e.g. `illustrative_v0`) is identical to its v0.1 behavior.

---

## 4. Guardrails — unchanged

Every v0.1 §10 guardrail holds verbatim. The disease populations are a **deepening of the
hypothesis-tier methodology view, never a step toward a verdict or a prediction**:

- **Tier D, always.** A disease-population assessment is capped at D and carries
  `NOT FOR PREDICTION`. The schema pins `predictive: false`.
- **Illustrative, not calibrated.** The records' notes state plainly that the magnitudes are
  illustrative heterozygous-scale shifts, not genotype-calibrated parameters, and that the
  output is a mechanism demonstration, not a per-patient or per-genotype risk claim.
- **No verdict.** The output remains a *distribution* of classifications and a susceptible
  fraction, with the healthy thresholds as the stated reference — never a "this drug is unsafe
  in LQT2" determination.

The tell that this layer has crossed its line: any output that reads as a patient- or
genotype-specific safety prediction. That does not get built.

## 5. Validation & verification contract (CI gates)

| Guard | What it proves | Mechanism |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Mean-shift correctness** | a population with `conductance_scale = {"IKr": k}` scales the kernel IKr conductance by `k` (times the CV draw) | unit test against the kernel |
| **Mechanism ordering** | against the healthy population, LQT2 prolongs APD most and the disease backgrounds lower qNet / raise the susceptible fraction — the textbook channelopathy ordering | property test |
| **Backward compatibility** | `illustrative_v0` (no `conductance_scale`) is byte-identical to v0.1; every record validates against the extended schema | full v0.1 suite + schema validation |
| **Tier / non-prediction** | every disease-population assessment is Tier D and stamped NOT FOR PREDICTION | assertion |
| **Dataset reproducibility** | the disease records regenerate byte-identically from `build_records.py` | CI `git diff --exit-code` |

*v0.3 adds the disease & genetic backgrounds the populations subsystem always promised, as the
minimal honest field — a per-current mean shift under the existing variability cloud — and
ships the three canonical long-QT channelopathies as illustrative, hypothesis-tier records. It
makes the gene–drug repolarization-reserve interaction visible and quantitative, and it remains,
like every Harmonia output, a distribution and not a verdict — and here, explicitly, never a
prediction.*
