# Harmonia — design spec extension v0.4: the cqInward inward-charge biomarker

**Status:** **implemented in v0.4.0** (2026-06-09) · extends [`spec.md`](../../spec.md) (v0.1)
· completes the computable half of the spec.md §3 `risk_metrics` list · **adds a diagnostic
readout, not a classifier; relaxes no §10 guardrail.**

> *spec.md §3 names six TdP-risk biomarkers — qNet, **cqInward**, EAD occurrence, APD90,
> triangulation, electromechanical window. Harmonia shipped qNet (the classifier), APD90,
> triangulation, and EAD detection. cqInward was named in the spec and the README but never
> actually computed. v0.4 implements it — the CiPA inward-charge biomarker — as an
> uncertainty-propagated diagnostic, and corrects the documentation.*

---

## 0. What cqInward is, and why it belongs

CiPA's mechanistic insight is that torsade is driven by the **balance between inward
(depolarizing) and outward (repolarizing) current** during the plateau: when inward charge
dominates, the plateau is sustained and early afterdepolarizations (the TdP precursor) become
possible. qNet captures the *net* of six currents. **cqInward** isolates the *inward* side —
the charge carried by the two main inward plateau currents, late sodium (INaL) and L-type
calcium (ICaL) — and reports how a drug shifts it relative to the drug-free control
([Dutta et al. 2017](../../dataset/citations/dutta-2017.json): "the cqInward metric quantifies
the change in the amount of charge carried by INaL and ICaL";
[Li et al. 2017](../../dataset/citations/li-2017.json)).

The mechanism it makes visible, and which validates it: a **pure ICaL blocker reduces**
inward charge (cqInward < 1 — protective, the multichannel mechanism that spares verapamil
and diltiazem), while a **pure IKr blocker prolongs the AP**, letting INaL/ICaL flow longer,
so it *raises* inward charge (cqInward > 1 — proarrhythmic). It is the inward-balance shift,
complementary to qNet's net view.

---

## 1. Definition

Per paced beat, the charge carried by current *X* is the time-integral of its trace over the
beat, `q_X = ∫ I_X dt`. Both INaL and ICaL are inward (negative) currents, so `q_NaL, q_CaL < 0`.
cqInward is the **control-normalized average of the two inward-current charge ratios**:

```
cqInward = ½ · ( q_NaL(drug) / q_NaL(control)  +  q_CaL(drug) / q_CaL(control) )
```

- `control` is the drug-free beat of the same AP-model variant (the memoised baseline).
- A drug-free assessment gives `cqInward = 1` by construction.
- `cqInward < 1` ⇒ the drug reduced inward charge (ICaL/INaL block dominates — protective).
- `cqInward > 1` ⇒ the drug increased inward charge (repolarization-current block prolonged
  the AP — proarrhythmic).

It is **dimensionless**, requires no kernel-specific threshold (the ratio is self-normalizing),
and is **propagated through the same Monte-Carlo as qNet**, so the assessment reports a
`cqinward_distribution` over the input variability, not just a point — consistent with the
project's thesis that every readout carries its input uncertainty.

---

## 2. Scope

**In scope** — the biomarker computation in the reference kernel (per-beat INaL/ICaL charges),
the control-normalized cqInward point + distribution on a `RiskAssessment`, and its display in
the CLI/summary, under both the moments and Bayesian (`uq`) uncertainty engines.

**Out of scope / honestly excluded** —

- **cqInward is a diagnostic, not the classifier.** The high/intermediate/low call stays with
  qNet (or ΔAPD90); cqInward is reported alongside, exactly as triangulation is. CiPA likewise
  qualified qNet as the decision metric and used the inward-charge feature as a mechanistic
  readout. Surfacing cqInward as a second verdict would violate §10.
- **EAD occurrence** remains detected but structurally unreachable in the reduced kernel
  (documented), and the **electromechanical window** needs a mechanical model (spec §2 exclusion).
  v0.4 completes the *computable* biomarkers; these two remain honestly out of reach.

---

## 3. Guardrails — unchanged

Every v0.1 §10 guardrail holds. cqInward is a distribution-valued mechanistic readout, never a
determination; it carries the same `clinicalUse = PROHIBITED` posture and propagates its input
uncertainty. No threshold is introduced (none is needed), so no calibrated classifier is added
or disturbed — the qNet/APD90 calibration and every recorded performance number are untouched.

## 4. Validation (CI gates)

| Guard | What it proves | Mechanism |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Control identity** | a drug-free / no-block assessment gives cqInward ≈ 1 | unit test |
| **ICaL-block sign** | a pure ICaL blocker gives cqInward < 1 (reduced inward charge) | kernel property test |
| **IKr-block sign** | a pure IKr blocker (AP prolongation) gives cqInward > 1 (increased inward charge) | kernel property test |
| **Uncertainty propagation** | the assessment reports a `cqinward_distribution` of the right shape under Monte-Carlo, and a finite point value | assess-level test |
| **Non-drift** | adding the biomarker changes no existing qNet/APD/flip number; the moments path stays byte-identical | full v0.1 suite |
